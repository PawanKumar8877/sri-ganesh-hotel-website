Welcome to Paradise

Your comfort and satisfaction is our priority

Experience Hospitality at Its Finest

Sri Ganesh Hotel & Restaurant is a premier destination offering world-class hospitality in Sahibganj, Jharkhand. Established with a vision to provide exceptional service, we have become the preferred choice for travelers seeking comfort, quality, and authentic Indian dining.

Our hotel features elegantly designed rooms with modern amenities, ensuring a comfortable and memorable stay. Our multi-cuisine restaurant serves delicious North Indian, South Indian, and Chinese dishes prepared by expert chefs using the finest ingredients.

Located in Kelabari Pokhariya, Azadnagar, we are easily accessible and close to major attractions. Whether you're visiting for business, leisure, or a family vacation, Sri Ganesh Hotel & Restaurant provides the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and culinary excellence.

We pride ourselves on our warm hospitality, attention to detail, and commitment to making every guest feel at home. Our dedicated staff works round the clock to ensure your stay is pleasant and hassle-free.
